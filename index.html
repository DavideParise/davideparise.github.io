About me

I am currently S.E.W. Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of California San Diego, mentored by Luca Spolaor. During Fall 2024, I was SLMath Postdoc at the Simons Laufer Mathematical Institute (formerly MSRI), taking part in the special program Special Geometric Structures and Analysis mentored by Prof. Tristan Rivière. Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the University of Cambridge, advised by Prof. Neshan Wickramasekera.

Research interests: Differential Geometry, Partial Differential Equations, Calculus of Variations, and Geometric Measure Theory.
